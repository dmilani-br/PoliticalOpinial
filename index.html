Manifesto

Russia × Ukraine

One of the most significant geopolitical conflicts of the 21st century, reshaping global security, energy markets, and diplomatic alliances.

The war influences economic stability, military strategies, and public perception across continents.

This platform observes how global sentiment reacts, without promoting narratives or political positions.

Observation, not influence.

Hamas × Israel

A long-standing conflict rooted in territorial disputes, political tensions, and deep humanitarian consequences.

Escalations impact regional stability and draw international attention and diplomatic responses.

Political Opinion observes public sentiment without endorsing sides or influencing outcomes.

Observation, not influence.

USA × China

An economic and technological rivalry shaping the global balance of power in the 21st century.

Trade, innovation, military influence, and diplomacy are central elements of this strategic competition.

The platform observes how global sentiment evolves amid shifting power dynamics.

Observation, not influence.

Iran × Iraq

A historic regional conflict with long-lasting political, religious, and strategic consequences.

Its legacy continues to influence Middle Eastern geopolitics and regional power structures.

This project observes how collective perception reflects these enduring tensions.

Observation, not influence.
